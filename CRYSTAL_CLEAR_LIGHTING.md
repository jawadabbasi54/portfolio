# Crystal-clear globe lighting update

Only the existing globe lighting and post-processing values were refined.

- Raised the bloom threshold and reduced bloom strength so city lights remain defined.
- Reduced tone-mapping exposure to restore night-side contrast.
- Restricted the warm city-light enhancement to bright city cores.
- Reduced cloud opacity and changed clouds from additive to normal transparency.
- Tightened the Fresnel atmosphere into a cleaner edge glow.
- Reduced glass-shell reflection opacity.
- Preserved the existing structure, routes, markers, controls, GSAP lifecycle, layout, and interactions.
